QR Information

Your Gateway to Historical Knowledge Through QR Codes

How It Works

  1. Find Informative QR codes at historical locations and monuments.
  2. Scan them with your smartphone's camera.
  3. Access detailed information about the place like photos, informative videos and historical facts instantly.

Features

  • Detailed Historical Information.
  • Photo Galleries.
  • Virtual Tours.
  • Detailed Informative Videos about the place with supportive Sign Languages for disabled's.
  • Documentries.
  • E-Books.
  • Key Historical Facts.
  • Information References.
  • User's Device Friendly Design.

Start exploring history by scanning QR codes at designated locations.
Each scan brings you comprehensive information about historical sites and monuments.
